Taylor Swift and Calvin Harris Have Been Texting Again

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Since Taylor Swift and Calvin Harris's split in June, there have been tweets, songs and very public rebounds. But now we have reports that they're communicating again, via text.

A source close to the couple tells TMZ that that shortly after Tom Hiddleston and Swift split earlier this month, Harris and Swift began texting and put an end to their drama. But don't get too excited, as reports from both sides say the texts are purely platonic and they still haven't spoken on the phone or seen each other in person.

Perhaps their newfound friendship is the reason why Swift posted a video of herself lip-syncing "This Is What You Came For" (the song she and Harris wrote together) a couple weeks ago. He even liked it.

Perhaps this will lead to more musical collaborations in the future? Even if they're not rekindling the flame of their relationship, being cool with your ex is definitely something worth cheering about.
