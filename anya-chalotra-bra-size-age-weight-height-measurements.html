Biography:

Anya Chalotra was born on 21 July 1996 in Wolverhampton, England. She attended the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art. She also studied at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama. Prior her television career, she started acting in theater. She has performed in numerous West End theatre productions.

In 2018, she made her first television role as Jennifer Ashman in the TV drama Wanderlust. At the same time, she appeared as Lily Marbury in 3 episodes of The ABC Murders. She also ventured into voice acting, playing Robin Loxley in the YouTube Premium series Sherwood. She recently gained mainstream recognition for her role as Yennefer of Vengerberg in Netflix’s The Witcher.
