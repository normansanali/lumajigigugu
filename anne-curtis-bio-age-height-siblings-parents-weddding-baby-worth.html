Anne Curtis Actress

Curtis’ career big break came in 2005 when she won the title role in the television series Kampanerang Kuba which marked her first collaboration with director Wenn V. Deramas. Most noteworthy, Anne received a nomination for Best Drama Actress at the PMPC Star Awards for TV.

Afterward, she co-starred with Oyo Boy Sotto in Sineserye Presents: May Minamahal. Furthermore, she likewise received a Gawad PASADO Award for Pinakapasadong Aktress nomination, a FAMAS Award for Best Actress nomination, a PMPC Star Award for Movies for Movie Actress of the Year nomination, as well as a Luna Award for Best Actress nomination.

In addition to this, Anne became one of the main hosts of the morning variety show, Showtime in 2009, and also starred in The Wedding, television series. Moreover, her projects in 2014 included her playing the title role in ABS-CBN’s adaptation of Mars Ravelo’s Dyesebel.

Nonetheless, Curtis launched her makeup line in the Philippines, BLK cosmetics in September 2017.
