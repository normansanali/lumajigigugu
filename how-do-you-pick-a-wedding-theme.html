    This question comes up often: “Do we need a wedding theme?” Strictly speaking, you don't need one – there's no box on the marriage license that you fill out “theme: rustic woodland” or “theme: enchanted garden”!

    How many colors should a bride choose? The standard rule is to choose a maximum of three colors unless you're going for an ombre or neutral look. Tie up your palette with a metallic color as an accent. If you go bringing more than three different colors together, your wedding will look like a riot.

    Should you match the wedding colors?

    The wedding colours are for the wedding party, not guests

    Unless you're in that bridal party, you'd be best to steer clear of trying to match them. Think of it a bit like a stage show. The actors, in this case the bridal party, all wear the same colour to identify themselves as being in the cast.
