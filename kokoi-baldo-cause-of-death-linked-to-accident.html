Who is Kokoi Baldo?

 Reggae singer Kokoi Baldo, who was a finalist in Season 2 of “The Voice of the Philippines,” died in a vehicular accident on Friday morning. He was 44.

Baldo’s wife, Sam, confirmed his death through Baldo’s official Facebook page, as she requested the public to delete any posts showing the accident scene. According to reports, the incident happened in Bacolod City.

Baldo was a four-chair turner during the blind audition of “The Voice of the Philippines” Season 2 in 2014, where he performed “One Day” by American reggae singer Matisyahu.

He joined the team of coach Sarah Geronimo and was eliminated from the singing competition before the second to the final round.

In a social media post after the news of his death, Geronimo thanked Baldo for sharing his talent in music.

The 44-year-old musician’s wife requested privacy during this difficult time and requested that posts about the accident scene be removed. Kokoi’s supporters were thanked for their patience. The reggae community mourns the loss of this gifted musician, who has left a legacy of music and memories.

Kokoi Baldo, a reggae singer, died in a reported car accident in Bacolod City. How Did Kokoi Baldo Die?

Reggae singer Kokoi Baldo died in a reported vehicular accident in Bacolod City. The details of the accident indicate that he lost control of his motorcycle while attempting to overtake a vehicle, resulting in a collision with a 10-wheeler truck carrying sugarcane. The impact of the accident led to his tragic death at the age of 44.

Kokoi Baldo Cause of Death 

Reggae singer Kokoi Baldo, real name Juan Manuel Ubaldo, died tragically in a car accident in Bacolod City, Philippines. Around 1:30 a.m. on Friday, the 44-year-old musician was attempting to overtake a vehicle on the Circumferential Road in Barangay Mandalagan.

Unfortunately, he lost control of his motorcycle and was killed when he was hit by a 10-wheeler truck transporting sugarcane.

The impact of the accident caused his head to separate from his body. The truck driver involved has been arrested. Kokoi Baldo’s death is being mourned by the reggae community, who will remember him for his musical contributions.
