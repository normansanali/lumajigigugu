Justin Bieber, Selena Gomez Reunite at Miami Recording Studio: Picture

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Old flames die really, really hard. Justin Bieber and perpetually on-and-off love Selena Gomez reunited once again at a Miami recording studio on Tuesday, April 8.

The pair tried to keep a low profile, as Bieber, 20, was photographed holding the door open for Gomez, 21. The "Boyfriend" singer dressed casually in a white t-shirt and oversized red hat while Gomez looked gorgeous in a white top and kept her brunette locks down.

The twosome were also joined by up-and-coming singer Madison Beer. Back in 2012, Beer, now 15, enjoyed major exposure when Bieber tweeted a link from her YouTube channel. Since getting noticed by the teen sensation, Beer has been working with Bieber's manager, Scooter Braun, and has signed with Island Def Jam.

Gomez appeared content during her late night studio stop by with Bieber, which comes just two weeks after getting served papers.
