TNA Wrestling Drops Dolph Zigglers First Merch After Hard To Kill Debut

Publish date: 2024-07-31

TNA Wrestling promised a big surprise at Hard to Kill, and they saw two former WWE Superstars debut for the company. Dana Brooke made her first appearance for the company, and the night ended with Dolph Ziggler showing up to show off.

Nic Nemeth showed up at TNA Wrestling’s Hard to Kill event. He made a big surprise ending for the show, as he took out the new TNA Champion Moose in the process.

TNA Wrestling did not waste any time putting out new merchandise for their big surprise. They did not confirm that Nemeth has inked a deal, but the t-shirt they put out certainly implies that there is a deal between the two.

Although it was noted that AEW wanted Dolph Ziggler, he is now with TNA Wrestling. He is also with NJPW, as he made his Wrestle Kingdom 18 debut previously this month.

TNA Wrestling inked deal with Xia Brookside in November prior to her TNA debut. There is no word on the contract status for Nic Nemeth or Dana Brooke. Obviously, this is a very interesting situation that could go on to help TNA out considerably.

We will keep our eye on this story, and so many more, here at Ringside News. Only time will tell what is next for Nic Nemeth as he continues his road after WWE.
