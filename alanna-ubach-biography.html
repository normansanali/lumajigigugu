Who is Alanna Ubach?

Alanna Ubach’s early life, childhood, and education

Ubach, born in California is the daughter of a Puerto Rican father and a Mexican mother. She was raised in Los Angeles along with her sister.

Since early childhood, Ubach began to show a conscious interest in artistic aspects. By the age of 7, she already had her debut as an actor in Romper Room.

And at 14, she started stage performance and also landed a role in the TV series named Beakman’s World.
