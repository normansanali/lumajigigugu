JENNIFER ANISTON AND JIM CARREY STEP OUT FOR 'BRUCE ALMIGHTY' PREMIERE

Publish date: 2024-08-06

    Hollywood stars Jim Carrey and Jennifer Aniston were on hand in support of their new film, Bruce Almighty, on Wednesday in Los Angeles.

    In the comedy, Jim plays Bruce, a TV reporter endowed with the divine powers of God, who is brought to the screen by actor Morgan Freeman. "I don't think anybody's ever done a movie where they're screaming at God," says the Liar Liar star.

    Jim says he and Jennifer, who plays his wife, have differing styles that work well together. "I'm someone who does all kinds of wild stuff, and she's like the centre of the wheel and I'm doing this all the way around her."

    The Friends actress had another way of describing working with the comedian, who is famed for his spontaneous physical comedy. She remembers: "I had bruises and rug burns and was thrown across the room."

    Meanwhile, the 34-year-old TV star, who received positive reviews for her turn in 2002's The Good Girl, revealed that in addition to her budding big screen career, she's looking forward to a blossoming family. "I'm ready for motherhood," she says. "I would love to start my family when Friends is done." She and hubby Brad Pitt, are apart for the moment, however, as he's shooting the epic Troy overseas.
