Who is Andie Paige? Lady Arrested for Dating the High School Student

Publish date: 2024-07-19

A 14-year-old student at her school was allegedly sexually assaulted by a Connecticut school lunch lady after she groomed him via text.

Andie Paige Rosafort, a former lunch assistant in New Fairfield, is accused of sending a teenage boy nude pictures and videos via Snapchat over the course of six months and engaging in sexual activity with him.

Rosafort, 31, is accused of asking the boy, “You want to see something?” before sending him her first naked picture.

After learning on January 17 that Rosafort and the teen had engaged in inappropriate behaviour, the New Fairfield Police Department started looking into the allegations.

The victim’s parents were informed by a friend, and they subsequently reported the alleged sexual assault to the police.

The affidavit states that Rosafort had been in touch with the victim for six months via Instagram and Snapchat among other social media sites.

The victim claimed that Rosafort sent him a message asking, “You want to see something?” before sending him a picture of himself undressed.

Rosafort sent a second uninvited nude selfie in response to the boy’s question about whether she might have sent the picture accidentally.

The woman is also accused of sending videos of herself masturbating to him over the course of several months, along with other sexts.

Rosafort is alleged to have demanded that the 14-year-old send her pictures of him in his undies as payment.
