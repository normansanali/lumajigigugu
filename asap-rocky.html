ASAP Rocky Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-10-02

Bio:
ASAP Rocky (born as Rakim Mayers on October 3, 1988) is an American rapper from Harlem, New York City, who started rapping at age 8. When ASAP was 12, his father went to jail in connection with selling drugs; he died in 2012. When ASAP was 13, his brother was killed in Harlem. The death inspired ASAP to take rapping more seriously. Rocky released his debut mixtape Live. Love. ASAP, in 2011 to critical acclaim. The success of his mixtape led to a record deal with Polo Grounds Music, RCA Records, and Sony Music Entertainment. He subsequently recorded his 2013 debut album Long. Live. ASAP, which was also well received by critics and debuted at number one on the Billboard 200.

Best Known For:
ASAP Rocky is best known for his mixtapes and songs such as “F**kin’ Problems.”

Personal Life:
ASAP Rocky began dating Australian female rapper Iggy Azalea in late 2011. Later, in July 2012, ASAP said they were no longer dating. ASAP Rocky then dated American model Chanel Iman, and has been on and off linked to Kendall Jenner since Feb. 2016.
