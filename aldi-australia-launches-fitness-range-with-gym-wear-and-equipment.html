Aldi Australia launches fitness range with gym wear and equipment

Publish date: 2024-07-25

A sellout portable blender, activewear from $7.99, memory foam joggers for $19.99 and a massage gun are among the new fitness range set to unveil at Aldi Australia. 

Shoppers looking to get fit for less can expect great deals on fitness gadgets, gym essentials and clothing on Saturday, January 30. 

The retailer’s Special Buys range includes a $49.99 swing stepper, $4.99 yoga mat, $9.99 fitness bra, $14.99 tights, and women’s or men’s joggers for just $19.99.
