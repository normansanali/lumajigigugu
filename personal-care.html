What Is a Rat Tail Comb?

A rat tail comb is a hair tool with a long stick at the very end of the comb. These combs help pull hair back and tease it. The pointy tail often comes in handy for opening knots or matted hair strands. They are commonly seen in salons and are essential for hairstylists.

Different Types of Rat Tail Combs

Rat tail combs come in different shapes and sizes. Depending on your hair type, the bristles of the comb section can be spaced far apart or very close together. In some cases, the rat tail is pointy and like a rod, while in others, it can be flat as well. Some rat tail combs have double-faced comb heads with fine bristles on one side and thick bristles on the other.

Uses of a Rat Tail Comb

Rat tail combs are one of the most common types of combs used in hairstyling. The most common uses for these combs are as follows:

  • You can use them to part your hair for various styles and looks.
  • They are also handy when trying to detangle a knot in your hair since they have flexible teeth.
  • Most people use them to tease the hair near the roots to increase volume.
  • You can also use it to hold sections of hair while ironing or trimming.

Tips for Using a Rat Tail Comb

A rat tail comb is a tool that can help you get the perfect hairstyle. This one-of-a-kind comb has thick teeth and wide spacing to help you style your hair faster. Here are some of the best tips for using it:

  • Comb the hair from the roots to the ends and then work your way up in small sections for better teasing.
  • Use the tail of the comb to make sections in your hair and pick up the sections that need to be parted.
  • Slightly oil the rat tail portion of the comb if you use it to open hair knots. This will prevent the hair from breaking.

    • Caution: Keep metallic rat tail combs dry and always sterilize them before use.

    What to Look for When Buying a Rat Tail Comb

    Rat tail combs are essential for your daily hair care routine. Hence, you should keep the following factors in mind when buying one.

    Material of the comb

    Most rat tail combs are made from plastic for the comb head and metal for the rat tail. Although plastic can cause static, it is a good option for people looking to tease hair. Rubber rat tail combs are more popular for detangling as they are more gentle on the hair. You can use wooden detangling combs if you have thick hair that does not work well with plastic.

    Length of the tail

    The length of the tail is significant for some styling techniques, as the tail also acts as a handle for the comb. Usually, longer tails make it easier to section and part the hair. They also easily lift a hair section when dyeing the hair.

    Type of bristles

    Rat tail combs usually have fine bristles that are very close to each other. This design helps users detangle hair to make it smooth. For curly and kinky hair, however, rat tail combs come with bristles that have a wider gap between the teeth. This is very useful for sectioning curly hair. You can also try using a comb with metal bristles to cut or trim curly hair.
