Geneva Ayala - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Best known as the former girlfriend of late rapper Xxxtentacion. She's been a controversial presence at various events after his death given the accusations of verbal and physical abuse she levied at him in 2016. 

Before Fame

She moved around a lot while she was a child having lived with her grandmother starting at the age of four, then with her mother at the age of 12 and then forced to move out on her own at age 16 after her mother kicked her out so the older woman could move in with a boyfriend. 

Trivia

She was raising money for a surgery to correct damage allegedly inflicted by the rapper, and after his death she reached her Go Fund Me goal of $25,000.

Family Life

Xxxtentacion passed away in June 2018. 

Associated With

Cleopatra Bernard is Xxxtentacion's mother.
