10 Top Shipping Companies in Nigeria

Publish date: 2024-08-08

A bulk part of the loads transported around the world today are transported through ships. This is more so due to the fact that most of the imports and exports between countries are done by sea. These goods range from crude oil to agricultural products, raw materials, mineral resources and finished products.

Nigeria also engage in a lot of importing and exporting. Nigeria majorly exports crude oils which she gets her major foreign venue from and at the same time however, she tend to import a lot of goods especially finished product.

Nigeria therefore, has a lot of shipping companies who are also involved in the shipping of goods, most of them offer great services as they all work with a field service app.

Some Top Shipping Companies in Nigeria Include:

Balaguer Limited

Balaguer Limited is a Nigerian private limited liability company. Their mission is to provide a total and innovative solution to meet the maritime component of international trade in order to ease the burden on importers of goods within the dynamics and legal framework of the ports administration in Nigeria. Balaguer Limited’s office is located on Aquarius Block, 2nd Floor, Eleganza Plaza, 1, Commercial Road Apapa.

Ayolas Cargo & General Services Limited

Ayolas Cargo and General Services Limited is a Nigerian company that offers shipping and freight services. Ayolas Cargo and General Services Limited is located on 129, Agege Motor Road, Alakija, Idi-Oro, Lagos

Antwerp Shipping Nigeria Limited

Antwerp shipping company is a shipping company in Nigeria which offers shipping of goods and cargoes. Antwerp Shipping Nigeria Limited is located on 1, Commerical Road, 1st Floor, Leo Block, Eleganza Plaza, Apapa.

Antonio Assaf& Sons (Nig.) Limited

Antonio Assaf& Sons (Nig.) Limited are one of the shipping companies in Nigeria’s maritime sector. Antonio Assaf& Sons (Nig.) Limited is located on 41/43 Bombay Crescent, Apapa, Lagos.

Alan Caray Technical Ltd

Alan Caray Technical Ltd is a one of the leaders among the shipping industries in Nigeria and has been carrying out shipping services in Nigeria for over 10 years. Alan Caray Technical Limited is located on 11, Wharf Road, Apapa, Lagos.

Air Sea Freighter Limited

Air sea Freighter Limited was incorporated in 1992. They however started operations in 1998. Their services include Shipping, Freighting (Clearning and Forwarding) Transportation and Consultancy amongst others. Air Sea Freighter Limited is located on 213, Aba Road, Opp. Shell RA, Trans Amadi, Port Harcourt.

African European Lines (Nig.) Limited

African European Lines Nigeria Limited is a Nigerian company which which offers shipping and freight services. African European Lines Nigeria Limited is located on 9 Crrek Road, Apapa, Lagos.

Advanced International Merchants Limited

Advanced International Merchants Limited was incorporated in 1995 and are involved in clearing and forwarding services. They also deal in automobile spare parts. Advanced International Merchants Limited is located on 22, Oduyemi Street, Anifowoshe, Ikeja.

3MO Shipping & Trading Company Limited

3MO Shipping & Trading Company Limited is an African shipping company and are involved in shipping and freight services. 3MO Shipping & Trading Company Limited is located on Aquarius Block, 1st Floor, 1 Commercial Road, Eleganza Plaza, Apapa, Lagos.
