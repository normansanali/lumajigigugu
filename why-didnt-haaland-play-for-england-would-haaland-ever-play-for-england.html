Why Didnt Haaland Play For England? Would Haaland Ever Play For England?

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Manchester City striker, Erling Haaland, didn’t play for England because he currently plays for Norway national team.

Would Haaland Ever Play For England?

Haaland may never play for England, even though he was born in England.

Who Is Erling Haaland?

Erling Haaland is a Norwegian  footballer who plays as a striker for Manchester City and the Norway national team.

Born on July 21, 2000, in Leeds, England, Haaland is one of the best athletes in the world.

Haaland has played for senior teams before he was absorbed into Molde in 2017.

The Austrian Bundesliga side Red Bull Salzburg signed him in 2019. In the same year, he won an Australian Cup plus two league titles.

Also in 2019, he played for German Bundesliga club Borussia Dortmund.

In 2019, Haaland represented Norway at the FIFA U-20 World Cup, scoring 9 goals and winning the tournament’s Golden Boot.

Haaland was a top scorer in five consecutive UEFA Champions League matches.

He had won the 2020 Golden Boy award, as well as the Bundesliga Player of the 2020 Season.
