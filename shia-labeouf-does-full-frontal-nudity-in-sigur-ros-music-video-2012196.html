Shia LaBeouf Does Full-Frontal Nudity in Sigur Ros Music Video

Publish date: 2024-07-31

It’s Shia LaBeouf in the buff!

On Monday, Icelandic indie band Sigur Ros released their new music video for “Fjogur Piano,” with 26-year-old LaBeouf baring all in the nearly nine-minute clip. “Icelandic people are not prudish about people being naked,” the band tells Just Jared of the “mystery film experiment.”

Georg Holm, bassist for the band, adds: “At first I didn’t know what to make of the video, because it is so full of ideas and confusion. Now I love it. It seems to have no beginning and no end, and just makes you think about what might and might not be going on.”

The music video was directed by Alma Har’el, and features a nude LaBeouf playing one half of a couple “locked in a never-ending cycle of addiction and desire,” according to Just Jared.
