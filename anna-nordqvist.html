Biography

Anna Nordqvist was born in Sweden on June 10, 1987. Swedish golfer who was named Rookie of the Year on the Ladies European Tour for 2009. She won her first major at the McDonald’s LPGA Championship that year and has gone on to win events like the 2010 European Ladies Golf Cup and the 2014 Kia Classic. She won the 2010 European Ladies Golf Cup and the 2011 Communitat Valenciana European Ladies Golf Cup with Sophie Gustafson. She was a hugely successful amateur golfer, winning the Swedish Amateur of the Year award in 2005. She was later named the Pac-10 Player of the Year in her freshman year at Arizona State University.
