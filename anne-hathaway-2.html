Anne Hathaway aka Anne Jacqueline Hathaway, is an American actress and model. As of 2023, Anne Hathaway’s net worth is $80 million. She is in the industry since the year 1999. Her films have earned more than $6.8 billion worldwide, and she was named to Forbes’ 2009 Celebrity 100 list.
