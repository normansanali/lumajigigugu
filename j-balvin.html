J Balvin Biography

J Balvin began his career at the age of 14, and after three years, he left his country and relocated to Oklahoma in the United States. He only lives there with his best friend, ‘Jesus Marulanda,’ to learn and practice English. After a few years, Jose relocated to New York to learn the fundamentals of music and English.

For the next ten years, Jose worked and tried to find the new style that people liked best so that he could build a platform for his career in both the United States and Columbia. He also performed in the best and most popular clubs in Medellin, Columbia, to begin connecting with the country’s people.

Career

In 2012, J Balvin released his first tape containing various mixed hit songs from Colombia. This mixtape contains the songs ‘Como un Animal’ and ‘En Lo Oscuro,’ which are very popular among the public. After a year, this mix trap goes viral in the United States, proving Jose’s worth as an industry radar and DJ.

The following year, Balvin released his trap song titled ‘6 AM,’ which is the featured song of Puerto Rican, and the song was also played on most radio stations in the United States, Europe, and Latin America. The second track, titled ‘Ay Vamos’ in the United States, helped Joes gain popularity in the Latin urban market. People prefer to listen to Latin music over other types of music, and this song topped the Billboard Latin song chart in 2015.

Balvin also canceled his first live telecast performance on Miss USA in June 2015 to protest Donald Trump’s insulting remarks about immigrants bringing crime, drugs, and rape activity to the United States. He also started the ‘LatinosSomosUnidos’ campaign on social media to protest all of the activities on the Venezuelan-Colombian border. The majority of Latin artists have supported the movement and are protesting alongside them.

On January 16, Jose launched his new Ginza for the latest album, which received two million views in 24 hours. In 2017, he also released his most recent studio album, which features Willy William. In the month of August 2017, ‘Mi Gente’ ranked first on Spotify’s top 50 songs list.

Achievements and Awards

Balvin has been nominated for several music awards every year, and he has also won the majority of Latin music awards. In 2017, he won Latin rhythmic song as well as an artist of the year at the Billboard Latin Music Awards. He was named best video and best artist of all time at the Heat Latin Music Awards in 2015 and again this year.

Aside from that, he was nominated for Latin album of the year at the 2017 iHeart Music Awards a few months ago. Balvin is now considered a Favorite Urban Artist in the Latin Music American Awards nominations.
