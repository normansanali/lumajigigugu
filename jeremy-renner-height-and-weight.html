Jeremy Renner height and weight

Publish date: 2024-07-27

A musician and actor from America, Jeremy Lee Renner was born on January 7, 1971.

Initially, he played supporting roles in smaller independent films like Dahmer (2002) and Neo Ned (2005) before moving on to larger productions like S.W.A.T. (2003) and 28 Weeks Later (2007). In 2008, Renner’s performance in The Hurt Locker earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor. He was also nominated for Best Supporting Actor for his work in The Town.

Beginning with the 2011 film Thor, Renner played Clint Barton/Hawkeye, a member of the Avengers. This led to the central title role in the Disney+ series Hawkeye (2021). He also acted in the films Mission: Impossible – Ghost Protocol (2011), The Bourne Legacy (2012), Hansel & Gretel: Witch Hunters (2013), American Hustle (2013), Mission: Impossible – Rogue Nation (2015), and Arrival (2016).

Father Lee Renner, who ran the Modesto bowling alley McHenry Bowl in the 1980s, and mother Valerie Cearley (née Tague) gave birth to Renner in Modesto, California. His parents wed while they were teenagers and separated when he was ten.

How tall is Jeremy Renner?

He is 178 cm  tall.

How much does Jeremy Renner weigh?

He weighs 181 lbs.
