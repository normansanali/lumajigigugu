Hailey Bieber Shuts Down Rumors About Marriage Problems With Justin Bieber Amid Her Sister's Arrest

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Highlights

  • Hailey sets the record straight about her marriage with Justin Bieber, shutting down false rumors with a sweet birthday post.
  • Stephen Baldwin's prayer request for Justin and Hailey takes new meaning after Alaia's nasty arrest incident.
  • Despite Alaia Baldwin's arrest for assault and battery, Hailey's family remains strong while dealing with the incident.

There have been plenty of rumors involving Hailey and Justin Bieber since the couple got married, but despite everything, their marriage seems to be going strong. Recently, once again, fans started to worry about the state of their relationship. This time, due to an unfortunate comment from Hailey's father, in which he asked for "a little prayer" for the couple. Hailey wasn't happy about the rumors, and took advantage of Justin's 30th birthday to set the record straight. Things are perfectly fine between them.

That is not the case for the rest of Hailey's family, however. Only a few days ago, Hailey Bieber's sister Alaia Baldwin was arrested for assault and battery, following an embarrassing incident during a night out with friends. Apparently, the concerns for the family were misplaced.

Hailey Bieber Posted Sweet Video With Justin That Shut Down Rumors

In honor of Justin Bieber's 30th birthday, Hailey Bieber made a very sweet post on Instagram, celebrating their love. She posted several pictures and short videos of the couple, including a picture of their wedding day and an old photo of Justin as a baby.

"30!?" the model wrote in the caption, "that was fast. Words could never truly describe the beauty of who you are. Happy Birthday to you... love of my life, for life."

This post's timing was fortunate, as fans were quick to point out in the comments. Lately, rumors have been going around about alleged marital problems between Justin and Hailey, and the singer's birthday was the perfect opportunity for them to show the world that they are as solid and in love as ever.

The rumors sparked when Hailey Bieber's dad, Stephen Baldwin, reposted a video on his Instagram story with an unsettling caption. The video featured Justin Bieber singing "I Could Sing of Your Love Forever" by Delirious? and Hillsong Worship, and the message below read:

"Christians, please when you think of Justin & Hailey, take a moment to offer a little prayer for them to have wisdom, protection and to draw close to the Lord."

Hailey Bieber's Sister Alaia Baldwin's Arrest Drama

After the news of Alaia Baldwin's arrest broke, Stephen Baldwin's request took a different meaning. It might not have alluded to marital problems in the Bieber household after all.

Although the news was made public just days ago, the incident which led to Alaia's arrest reportedly happened on February 24th, only a few days before her dad's public request for a prayer. Hailey's sister was booked for assault and battery, and while the incident was somewhat violent, it mostly stood out for being nasty.

"Her friends said she needed a minute and she said, 'I need to change my tampon,'" the bartender recalled. "She proceeded to stay in the bathroom. I told her again she had to leave and she said, 'I'm changing my tampon, do you want to see it?' And flings it up and it smacks my face."

Things allegedly escalated after this disgusting development. The police were called, and Alaia Baldwin was taken to jail. The Bieber-Baldwin family have yet to speak about this, but they will undoubtedly need each other's support during this difficult time.
