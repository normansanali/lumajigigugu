What cars does Tyler the Creator own?

Publish date: 2024-09-18

Tyler Gregory Okonma is well-known professionally as Tyler, the Creator, and he is an American rapper, singer, and record producer. He is one of the founding members of the music collective Odd Future. Okonma has won two Grammy Awards, three BET Hip Hop Awards, a BRIT Award, and an MTV Video Music Award.

Okonma has created cover art for Odd Future’s releases, their clothing, and other merchandise as a visual artist. In 2011, Okonma founded the streetwear brand Golf Wang and then began the annual music festival Camp Flog Gnaw Carnival in 2012. Okonma also founded and operated the streaming service Golf Media between 2015 and 2018, which contained original scripted series from Tyler and a live stream of each Camp Flog Gnaw Carnival.
