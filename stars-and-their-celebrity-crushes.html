Stars and Their Celebrity Crushes

Publish date: 2024-09-30

Celebrities are just like Us — they get crushes on famous folks too.

Amy Adams admitted during a November 2020 appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live! that she developed a “retroactive crush” on Robert De Niro after watching him in old movies including The Godfather and The Deer Hunter.

“Meaning, like, I have a crush on him in the ’70s,” Adams explained. “I mean, not that I wouldn’t have a crush on him now, but you know, I’m married.”

Celebrity crushes are not limited to pining from afar. Emma Watson, who played Hermione in the Harry Potter franchise, revealed that she was smitten with costar Tom Felton (who played Draco Malfoy) early in their friendship.
