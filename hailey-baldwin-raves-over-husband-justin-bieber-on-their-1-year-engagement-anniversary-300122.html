Hailey Baldwin Raves Over Husband Justin Bieber On Their 1 Year Engagement Anniversary

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Believe it or not, it’s already been a year since Justin Bieber asked Hailey Baldwin to marry him and she said yes! To celebrate the milestone, the model took to social media to post a sweet pic and dedicated a message to her now-husband and their love.

‘1 year ago I said yes to being your best friend for life, and today I’ve never loved you more. Life gets more beautiful every single day because of you, my heart belongs to you forever. Here is to learning and growing together,’ Hailey wrote in the caption of the snap that featured her and Justin on a mountain together.

In the pic, Justin wears a pair of green shorts and no shirt while also rocking a teal hat.

He is looking away from Hailey who wore a teal, grey and brown jacket and paired it with brown cowboy boots.

Hailey posed by leaning on her husband with her eyes closed.

The model’s celebratory post comes almost a week after Justin got in a bit of hot water for defending manager Scooter Braun amid his war with Taylor Swift.

Even Hailey’s support to her husband got some hate since she wrote ‘gentleman’ in the comment section and people were quick to argue that his post was not exactly gentleman-like.
