Pusha T and Jay Z Drop Ominous New Track Drug Dealers Anonymous

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Just when you thought that annoying “Damn Daniel” meme was tired and played-out, Jay Z mentions it in a verse and suddenly it lights up the internet. Pusha T and Jay Z, who is still working on that Lemonade response, collaborated on a new track, “Drug Dealers Anonymous,” which features a sample of conservative pundit Tomi Lahren’s rant about Beyoncé and Jay Z (“Your husband was a drug dealer … for 14 years he sold crack cocaine”). The song will appear on Pusha T’s upcoming album King Push. Lahren quickly tweeted a very eloquent response to her unwitting cameo:

Always wanted to be on a track with two drug dealers. Thanks @PUSHA_T & #JayZ #DrugDealersAnonymous

— Tomi Lahren (@TomiLahren) June 1, 2016 Hear Pusha T/Jay Z’s ‘Drug Dealers Anonymous’
