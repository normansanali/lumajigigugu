Barbara Tennor And Jerry Appiah

Publish date: 2024-09-30

After a lot of speculation, Love is Blind’s Kwame Appiah finally thought to speak about his experience on the show and his relationship with Chelsea Griffin. Social media people have been kind criticizing of him speaking out loud that he does not even love his fiancé. So much so that he also has been trolled for faking about his family. The family part, let us tell if it is true or not in this writing about his siblings: Barbara and Jerry.

Love Is Blind: Who Are Who Are Kwame Appiah’s Siblings?

On Love Is Blind, Kwame Appiah mentioned a brother named Jerry Appiah and a sister, Barbara Tennor. But, the internet chose not to believe it, especially about Barbara.

At first, viewers loved to see Kwame’s sister. They even thought her to be the “best part” of the contestant. They loved seeing her supportive and positive energy at Kwame and Chelsea’s wedding. But, on the 10 April 2023 episode, people took to the internet to gossip that Barabara is not Kwame’s real sister. They swore that he hired “a random Ghanaian woman” to play his sister.

Not stopping there, people also berated Kwame for having “fake” phone calls with his mother, who seemed to be against her son getting married on Love Is Blind.

Anyway, let us tell you more about Kwame’s siblings in the rest of the writing.

Meet Barbara Tennor, Kwame Appiah Sister

During the Love Is Blind Season 4 reunion on 16 April 2023, the program’s co-host helped Kwame Appiah put the internet chatter to rest, noting that his sister Barbara is not a paid actress. Kwame agreed of course and additionally praised her for having helped raise him. He said she has been his best friend his whole life.

Kwame’s wife and co-star Chelsea also gave her own thought that Barbara is a good sister and a fairy Godmother of a sister.

On the show, viewers have also seen Chelsea get close to Kwame’s mom and his nieces.

  • Barbara Tennor Age

Because Barbara Tennor was born in October 1984, she turned 38 years old in 2022.

  • Barbara Tennor Job

Per LinkedIn, Barbara Tennor is a hospital and healthcare professional. Here, she mentioned working as unit security at Frederick Memorial Healthcare Systems from 2006 until now.

Before this, she did the likes of jobs as a child care worker, medication administrator, personal care assistant, peer counselor, and teacher’s aide. Even prior, she did her Master’s in Health Administration at the University of Maryland Global Campus from 2010 – 2014. Also, between 2005 and 2010, she studied B.S. in Biology at Mountain State University.

  • Is Barbara Tennor Married?

Barbara Tennor’s marital status was not known at the time of this writing.

Meet Jerry Appiah, Kwame Appiah Brother

One can tell Jerry Appiah is really fond of and proud of his brother Kwame Appiah and vice-versa.

  • Jerry Appiah Age

Jerry Appiah was born in May 1982. So, he turned 40 years old in 2022.

So, Kwame happens to be the youngest in the family, with a decent age gap with both his siblings. By March 2023, he was 32 years of age.

  • Jerry Appiah Job

Jerry Appiah is a data analyst working full-time at Berkeley County Council in West Virginia, United States from November 2020 until the time of this writing.

On LinkedIn, he mentions being a driven IT practitioner dedicated to offering his expertise to produce exceptional outcomes. He claims to be an insightful professional with a keen ability to learn, evaluate and assess company needs to fit evolving technology standards.

He studied at Quince Orchard High School and the University of Maryland, College Park.

  • Is Jerry Appiah Married?

Indeed. Jerry Appiah is married. He has a wife named Lacey Appiah, who according to her Facebook is an HR Business Professional, full-time senior recruiter at PeopleScout in Maryland, United States, and licensed real estate agent at Keller Williams Realty Centres. Back in the day, she studied Business Administration, Management, and Operations at the University of Phoenix (2008 – 2010).

Later, on 22 April 2017, she and Jerry got married. And the next year on March 30th, their son was born. Kwame, who is just as much fond of him, took to his social media to write “Haven’t gotten to hype this fact yet. My first nephew was born only several weeks ago. Congratulations to my brother on the baby, and the family for a new KING!”.
