Who is Louis Nitsche?

Nitsche is an actor from Germany, and according to Wikipedia, he has worked in two of the Netflix creations; You, He & We (2021) and For Jojo (2022).

The German actor also has feature credits in the TV mini-series Hit and Run (2018), the movie Isi & Ossi (2020), Ivie wie Ivie (2021), and The Four of Us (2021). Before signing into prominent roles with reputed production companies, it was all minor and interim roles on TV for Nitsche. His first leading appearance on TV was in the ZDF crime series SOKO Köln where he portrayed the role of Tom Rüttgers. 

After that, he was cast in a few more ZDF series, such as Notruf Hafen Kante, Die Chefin, and SOKO Munich, playing episodic roles. While being frequent in ZDF productions, he also got the opportunity to play an episode leading role on Bettys Diagnose.
