Chrissy Teigen Trolls John Legend for Oscars Loss

Publish date: 2024-09-12

The internet's favorite couple, Chrissy Teigen and John Legend, stole the show on this year's Oscars red carpet with all their ridiculously adorable couple moments.

Despite winning cutest couple of the night, Legend didn't take home the Oscar for "Best Song" this year—and Teigen isn't about to let her husband forget that. Twitter's queen of sarcasm spent yesterday roasting Legend over his Oscar loss this year.

On Snapchat, she introduced him by saying, "Hey guys, here joined by Oscar winner John Legend...not this year!"

Then, when a Hollywood tour bus drove past Teigen and Legend, she shouted at the screaming tourists out the window, "It's John Legend, he won an Oscar...not this year though!!"

Of course, Legend was a good sport about it and even joined in with her adding, "Not this year!"

This is how you keep your significant others humble and in check, people.
