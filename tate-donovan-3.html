A director and actor, Tate Buckley Donovan, born on September 25, 1963. He is known for his role played in the film named ‘Love Poison No.9’ in which he acted alongside Sandra Bullock in 1992. Among his other acting credits, Tom Shayes in Damages, Jimmy Cooper in The O.C., the voice of Hercules in the 1997 Disney animated film of the same name, and a few Kingdom Hearts video games.

Career

The most acclaimed actor, Tate Donovan established his career in multiple fields, such as; acting, production, and direction. He is known for his wonderful work and performances for which he always gets appreciation from his fans and the viewers.

He has played supporting roles in films including Good Night, Good Luck, and Argo. Among others, he also played Brian Sanders in Hostages and White House Chief of Staff Mark Boudreau in 24: Live Another Day.

Moreover, Donovan also appeared as a guest in a number of television series titled ‘Notably Friends’. He then earned a nomination for the Independent Spirit Award for the category of Best Supporting Male for his Outstanding Performance in the film titled ‘Inside Monkey Zetterland’.

However, the actor has made appearances in a variety of movies and television series for which he gained immense popularity in his career life. And that’s what he calls a big win.

Achievements

Tate Donovan also worked as a producer of ’30 for 30 Shorts’ for which he was awarded the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Short Form Nonfiction Or Reality Series.
