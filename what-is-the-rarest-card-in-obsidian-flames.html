What is the rarest card in obsidian flames?

Publish date: 2024-07-06

The Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) has always been home to various rare and sought-after cards that capture the attention of collectors and players alike.

In the latest expansion, Obsidian Flames, there are several standout cards, each with its unique appeal. However, one question that often arises is: What is the rarest card in Obsidian Flames? 

Understanding Rarity in Obsidian Flames

Obsidian Flames is a set known for its top-heavy distribution, primarily due to the presence of the iconic Pokémon, Charizard.

The set's high supply, driven by the popularity of Charizard cards, has led to a significant variance in the value of different cards within the set.

This has resulted in some surprising revelations about which cards stand out as the rarest and most valuable in the set.

Special Illustration Rare Charizard ex

One of the most sought-after and rare cards in Obsidian Flames is the Special Illustration Rare (SIR) Charizard ex.

This card stands out not only for its stunning artwork by AKIRA EGAWA but also for its gameplay significance.

With a 0.5% pull rate, the SIR Charizard ex is objectively low in supply, making it a highly coveted and rare find in booster packs.

Competitive Significance

Unusually, this Charizard card is exceptionally strong in competitive play, featuring high damage attacks, relevant abilities, and a strategic advantage in the metagame.

Its competitive prowess has contributed to its high demand and value, with even the Double Rare version costing double digits for a playset due to overwhelming fan interest.

The Charizard Effect

The phenomenon known as "The Charizard Effect" has played a significant role in elevating the value and rarity of Charizard cards in Obsidian Flames.

The widespread belief that any card featuring Charizard holds substantial value has led to an inflation of its perceived worth, even for cards classified as "bulk" under normal circumstances.

Rarity and Value Comparison

While the SIR Charizard ex holds significant rarity and value, it's essential to compare it to other cards in the set to gain a comprehensive understanding of its position as the rarest card in Obsidian Flames.

Hyper Rare Charizard ex

Another highly sought-after rarity in the set is the Hyper Rare Charizard ex. This card stands out for its vivid illustration and exceptional gameplay attributes, making it one of the rarest Pokémon cards in Obsidian Flames.

With 330 HP and 180 base damage for its Burning Darkness move, it's a force to be reckoned with, especially in the late game, contributing to its high value.

Pidgeot ex and Gloom

Interestingly, the Special Illustration Rare version of Pidgeot ex has garnered significant attention from both collectors and players due to its appeal in the meta, leading to a surge in its price.

Conversely, the seemingly unremarkable Gloom card has defied expectations by fetching a price that is more than double that of other cards of similar rarity, despite its limited gameplay impact.

 

All in all, the rarest card in Obsidian Flames is undeniably the Special Illustration Rare Charizard ex. Its combination of low pull rate, competitive significance, and the pervasive "Charizard Effect" has cemented its position as the most sought-after and valuable card in the set.

Although, it's crucial to acknowledge the unique appeal and value of other cards within the set, such as the Hyper Rare Charizard ex and the surprising price dynamics observed with cards like Pidgeot ex and Gloom.

In this Fan Arch piece, we've explored the intricacies of rarity in Obsidian Flames, shedding light on the factors that contribute to the value and desirability of specific cards.

Whether you're a collector aiming to secure the rarest cards or a player seeking strategic advantages, understanding the dynamics of rarity in the TCG can provide valuable insights and enhance your overall experience with the game.
