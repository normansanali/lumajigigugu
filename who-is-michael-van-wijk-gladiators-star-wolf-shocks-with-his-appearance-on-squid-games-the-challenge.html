What is 'Squid Games: The Challenge' all about?

'Squid Games: The Challenge' is based on Netflix's OG series 'Squid Games.' Some of the challenges in the games are almost the same as that of the original show.

The first challenge was "red light green light." It showed the contestants running towards the red line in order to move ahead in the show.

However, the show took a turn when the "tug of war" challenge was replaced by the "warship" challenge. As the program progressed, numerous contestants were eliminated from the game.

Towards the end, we will see only a few contestants remaining, who will then fight physically as well as emotionally to win the game and the huge prize money. With the elimination of each contestant, $100K money is added to the winning prize.
