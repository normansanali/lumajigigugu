How Tall Is Bronny James? All You Need To Know About Lebron James First Son

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Bronny James might not be as popular as his superstar father, Lebron but he is definitely living off his fame as an upcoming basketball player which is why people are interested in knowing more about him beyond just being the son of one of the greatest players of all time. He is also looking forward to playing in the NBA where his father currently plays as a Small Forward for the Los Angeles Lakers. This post takes a look at the background, education, career among other details about Bronny James.

Who is Bronny James?

LeBron James Jr., commonly known as “Bronny,” is a budding American basketball talent enrolled as a freshman at the University of Southern California (USC). Born on October 6, 2004, in Cleveland, Ohio, he is the eldest son of the renowned LA Lakers Superstar, LeBron James, who is widely acclaimed as one of the greatest players in basketball history. Following in his father’s footsteps, Bronny aspires to pursue a professional basketball career and make a mark in the NBA.

Beyond the basketball court, Bronny is an enthusiastic gamer, affiliated with the Esports team FaZe Clan. He is actively engaged on social media platforms such as TikTok and Twitch, amassing a substantial following. Notably, Bronny boasts 4.3 million followers on TikTok and over 300,000 followers on Twitch. At the age of 19, standing at 6 feet 4 inches, he currently plays the role of a point guard, occasionally taking on the position of a shooting guard, for the University of Southern California’s basketball team. He is very likey to be drafted into the NBA in the years ahead. He nurses the dream of playing alongside his father before he retires. Lebron James is currently 38 years old.

Background

LeBron “Bronny” James Jr., the eldest son of NBA superstar LeBron James and his wife Savannah Brinson, was born on October 6, 2004, in Akron, Ohio. His parents, LeBron James and Savannah Brinson, were 19 and 18 years old, respectively, at the time of his birth. While growing up, Bronny engaged in various sports, though his father prohibited him from participating in football and ice hockey due to safety concerns. Despite his natural athleticism, basketball always remained his primary interest, although he also dabbled in soccer during his early years.

In 2014, at the age of 9, a basketball highlight reel showcasing Bronny James’ remarkable skills gained widespread attention. The fact that he was LeBron James’ son only amplified the video’s national recognition, sparking extensive discussions about his potential as a player. Over the years, Bronny has been part of multiple AAU basketball teams, and he currently serves as the Point Guard for the USC Trojans basketball team.

How Tall Is Bronny James?

LeBron James Jr. is 19 years old and is listed at 6’4″ as of 2023. He has the ideal height as a prospective shooting or point guard in the NBA.

Which High School did Bronny James play for?

Bronny James attended Sierra Canyon School, a private institution located in Los Angeles, California, where he displayed his talents on the court.

Education

Bronny James initially enrolled at Old Trail School in Bath, Ohio, where he spent his first two years of high school. Subsequently, in 2018, he made a move to Crossroads School for Arts & Sciences in Santa Monica, California. His educational journey continued in 2019 when he transferred to Sierra Canyon School located in Chatsworth, California.

Net Worth

Bronny James net worth is estimated to be in the region of $5m. He is an upcoming American basketball athlete. Renowned as the eldest son of LeBron James, Bronny earned recognition as a consensus four-star recruit and achieved the status of a McDonald’s All-American during his senior year in high school in 2023. His burgeoning career has seen him secure multiple endorsement agreements, with notable brands such as Nike, Sprite, and Panini. Bronny James Cardiac Arrest

On July 25, 2023, Bronny James, the elder son of NBA star LeBron James, experienced a cardiac arrest during a basketball practice session at the University of Southern California. A family spokesperson confirmed his hospitalization on Monday.

As of the latest update, Bronny has been moved out of the intensive care unit and is now in stable condition, as mentioned in the official statement.

“Yesterday, during basketball practice, Bronny James suffered a cardiac arrest. Prompt medical intervention was administered, and he was subsequently taken to the hospital. We are relieved to report that he is now in stable condition and has been moved out of the ICU,” the statement read:

“The James family requests respect and privacy during this time, and further updates will be provided to the media as more information becomes available.”

“LeBron and Savannah express their heartfelt gratitude to the USC medical and athletic staff for their exceptional efforts and commitment to the well-being of their athletes.”

At 19 years old, Bronny is an incoming freshman for USC’s basketball team, having graduated from Sierra Canyon High School in Los Angeles earlier this spring. Standing at 6 feet 3 inches, the versatile guard earned a four-star recruit rating and showcased his skills in the McDonald’s All-American Game in March, featuring some of the nation’s top high school basketball players.

Siblings

LeBron “Bronny” James Jr. has two siblings: Bryce Maximus, aged 16, and Zhuri Nova, aged 9. Bronny and Bryce were born prior to their parents’ wedding in 2013, while Zhuri joined the family a little over a year after the significant celebration.

Bronny James Health

Lebron James has provided an update on the health of his son, Bronny after the cardiac arrest incident in August. Over the summer, the college basketball player experienced cardiac arrest due to a congenital heart defect. According to Lebron: “Bronny is doing extremely well. He’s began his rehab to get back on the floor this season,” LeBron said at the time, excited to see “where he is and what his future still has in store for him.”
