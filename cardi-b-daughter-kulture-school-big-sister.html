Cardi B and Offset Share Pics of Kulture's Days After She Became a Big Sister

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Cardi B has sent her daughter, Kulture, off to school for the first time.

The rapper and her husband, Migos member Offset, shared adorable pics of the 3-year-old as she went to school this week. In one of the shots, Kulture kisses her father on the cheek while standing by a car. The toddler is dressed in a stylish olive green jacket over a white turtleneck and a matching green skirt. She also has her braids tied back and holds a Disney Princess-themed rolling backpack.

"KK first day," Offset wrote.

Cardi also posted her daughter's second-day outfit on the account she maintains for Kulture on Friday. In this pic, the 3-year-old poses with her hand on her hip as she rocks a denim jacket, white shirt, dark pants, and white sneakers. She also showed off her love of Disney yet again with a Frozen lunchbox.

The cute outfit pics are our first glimpse of Kulture as a new big sister, posted just days after Cardi gave birth to a newborn son. The baby boy is the rap couple's second child together. Offset also has three children–sons Kody, 6, and Jordan, 11, and daughter Kalea, 6–from previous relationships.

The "Bodak Yellow" rapper, 28, revealed the news Monday with a photo of herself and Offset in the delivery room, where both of the rappers look lovingly at the new addition to their family.

The couple said in a statement, "We are so overjoyed to finally meet our son. He is already loved so much by family and friends and we can't wait to introduce him to his other siblings."
