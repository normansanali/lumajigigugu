Britney Spears Spotted Out And About With Boyfriend Paul Soliz

Britney Spears was seen driving around with Paul Soliz, just days after she was reportedly involved in a violent fight with him that left her injured.

The insider added that the pop star was "acting weird" and "yelling" at her boyfriend to "leave her alone."

Apparently, the insider wasn't the only one who noticed something was wrong between the pair. "A few guests called the front desk to alert them about the problem because they knew it was Britney Spears and thought she needed help," they said.

Britney Spears Admits She Got Injured, But Doesn't Blame Paul Soliz
